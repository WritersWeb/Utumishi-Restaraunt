About Us

Welcome to Utumishi Restaurant, where culinary tradition meets modern dining. Founded in 2010 by [Founder's Name], Utumishi began as a small family-run eatery with a simple goal: to share the rich flavors of authentic Kenyan cuisine with the local community. Over the years, our dedication to quality ingredients, traditional recipes, and warm hospitality has allowed us to grow into the beloved restaurant we are today.

At Utumishi, we believe in using fresh, locally sourced produce, meats, and spices whenever possible. Our chefs are passionate about preparing each dish with care and attention to detail, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience for every guest. From our flavorful Pilau and Nyama Choma to our comforting Mukimo and refreshing salads, our menu offers a diverse selection of traditional Kenyan favorites and modern culinary creations.

Our Mission

To provide an exceptional dining experience by serving delicious, authentic Kenyan cuisine in a welcoming and comfortable atmosphere, while supporting local farmers and sustainable practices.

Our Vision

To be the leading restaurant in the region, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and community engagement, while preserving the rich culinary heritage of Kenya.

More than just a restaurant, Utumishi is a place where friends and family gather to share good food and create lasting memories. We are committed to providing excellent service and creating a warm and inviting atmosphere for all our guests. We are open 7 days a week from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM and also offer catering services for special events. Contact us for more information.
